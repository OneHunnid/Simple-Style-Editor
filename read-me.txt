//WELCOME

Congrats and thank you for downloading the WebCards Simple Style Editor! We hope you enjoy this Squarespace extension and share with other Squarespace developers to spread the love.

// INSTALLATION

To use the Simple Style Editor you will need to log into your Squarespace developer account via SFTP and replace your base.less file WebCards base.less file.  This will switch the style sheet to ours and you will have a simple style editor to use for your site.
